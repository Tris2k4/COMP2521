10 keys: 1 12 23 34 45 56 67 78 89 100


(c) What is the worst-case search cost after N total items have been
    inserted? Justify your answer.

The worst case can be O(n) where n is the number of items in that particular index. It is O(n) because we have to traverse through the whole linked list, hence O(n)
